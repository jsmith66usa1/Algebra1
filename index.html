Algebra Overview

Algebra is a fundamental branch of mathematics that uses variables to represent numbers and solve problems. It's essentially the language of patterns and relationships, helping us to model and understand the world around us. This course will give you the tools to move from basic arithmetic to solving complex equations and understanding mathematical functions.

Course Session Summary

  • We start with the basics: variables, expressions, and the order of operations.
  • We'll then learn to solve linear equations and inequalities, moving from simple one-step problems to more complex multi-step and systems of equations.
  • The second half of the course focuses on more advanced topics like exponents, polynomials, quadratic equations, and radical expressions.
  • We finish with an introduction to functions and a comprehensive final review.
